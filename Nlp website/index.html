What is natural language processing (NLP)

Natural language processing (NLP) is the ability of a computer program to understand human language as it is spoken and written -- referred to as natural language. It is a component of artificial intelligence (AI).

Why NLP are in demand

Natural language processing helps computers communicate with humans in their own language and scales other language-related tasks.

Text classification
This involves assigning tags to texts to put them in categories
Text extraction
This involves automatically summarizing text and finding important pieces of data
Tokenization
This is when text is broken down into smaller units to work with.
Morphological segmentation
This divides words into smaller parts called morphemes
